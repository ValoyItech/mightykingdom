About Mighty Kingdom Funerals

Mighty Kingdom Funerals is committed to providing compassionate and dignified funeral services to families during their most difficult times.

With years of experience in the industry, we understand the importance of honoring your loved ones with respect and care.

Our team is dedicated to guiding you through every step of the process, ensuring that your needs are met with professionalism and empathy.

Terms & Conditions

Eligibility: The main member must be between 18 and 64 years old.

Payout Limit: If Mighty Kingdom Funerals fails to operate in the country, a payout of R1600 will be made.

Full Disclosure: All information must be disclosed truthfully when joining.

Waiting Period: There is a 6-month waiting period before activation.

Joining Fee: A once-off joining fee of R100 is required.

Cancellation: The policy is canceled if the main member passes away.

Transportation Fee: R8/km for delivery outside Limpopo, Gauteng, or Mpumalanga.

Suspension: Policy is suspended if premiums are not paid.

Monthly Payments: Premiums must be paid by the 1st of each month.
